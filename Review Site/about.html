Our Story

From garage tinkering to performance innovation

Who We Are

Chapman Performance was founded in 2015 by Andrew Chapman, a lifelong car enthusiast who grew up working on cars in his father's garage. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a respected name in the performance parts industry.

Our team consists of engineers, mechanics, and racing enthusiasts who understand what it takes to build high-performance vehicles. We're not just sellers - we're users of our own products, testing them on the track and street to ensure they meet our rigorous standards.

Our Mission

"To provide high-quality, innovative performance parts that help enthusiasts get the most out of their vehicles without compromising reliability or breaking the bank."

- Andrew Chapman, Founder

Our Journey

2015

Company Founded

Andrew starts building custom suspension components in his garage for local racers.

2017

First Product Line

Launch of our signature coilover series, quickly gaining popularity in the grassroots racing community.

2019

Expansion

Moved to our current 10,000 sq ft facility and added turbo systems to our product lineup.

2022

National Recognition
